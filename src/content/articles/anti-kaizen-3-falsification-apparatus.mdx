# The Falsification Apparatus

*The military period didn't just commit atrocities and conceal them. It built administrative systems to make honest accounting structurally impossible — in real time. The postwar settlement left those systems largely intact.*

---

The Meiji construction disabled Japan's social-domain feedback mechanisms by doctrine. The militarist period took the next step: it built **falsification into operations themselves**.

---

## Falsification in Real Time

With the comfort women system and the conduct of the China campaigns, atrocities were not simply committed and then retrospectively concealed. The administrative systems were designed from the beginning to prevent accurate accounting.

Records were destroyed during operations, not after. Euphemistic language was institutionalised at every bureaucratic level — the vocabulary itself made honest description structurally difficult. Units operated under verbal rather than written orders precisely to avoid documentation.

The thought control apparatus reinforced this from above. Academics who questioned official narrative were imprisoned. The space in which honest assessment could occur was eliminated — not just discouraged.

This is anti-kaizen at maximum intensity: not merely refusing to examine defective outputs, but **deliberately disabling the defect detection system itself** as the process ran.

By 1945, Japan was operating nationally with no functioning mechanism for reality to correct the official narrative — which is part of why the war continued long past the point any honest assessment would have recommended ending it.

---

## The Settlement That Didn't Settle

When MacArthur's occupation administration retained Emperor Hirohito and shielded him from war crimes prosecution, the reasoning was pragmatic. The Cold War made Japan strategically indispensable. The price was that **Japan never had its Nuremberg**.

Germany's reckoning was thorough. Nuremberg established in legal and public record precisely what occurred and who was responsible. It created the foundation on which all subsequent German historical culture was built.

Japan's Tokyo Trials prosecuted military leaders but left the ideological and bureaucratic infrastructure largely intact. The ministries that had administered wartime propaganda administered postwar reconstruction. The educational bureaucracy that had enforced State Shinto nationalism managed postwar curriculum.

The Meiji construction — the elevation of national narrative above truth obligation — was **never formally dismantled**. It was merely quieted.

---

## The Continuity

The LDP's nearly unbroken governance from 1955 onward ensured the quieting never became dismantling. No equivalent of Willy Brandt's generation — politicians whose identity was defined by rupture with the preceding era — came to power with a different relationship to the past.

Kishi Nobusuke, a Class A war crimes suspect, served as Prime Minister from 1957 to 1960. He was the grandfather of Abe Shinzō, whose governments produced the most aggressive historical revisionism of the postwar era. The postwar settlement was built by people with personal interest in the shape of the historical narrative.

The Meiji construction was never dismantled because the people best positioned to dismantle it were precisely the people most invested in its preservation.
